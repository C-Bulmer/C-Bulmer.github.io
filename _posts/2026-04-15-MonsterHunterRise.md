  Monster Hunter Rise is the 2nd game in 5th generation of Monster Hunter Games, following up the very popular Monster Hunter: World. Rise was my first introduction to the series. I completed the low rank initally, and after beating Monster Hunter Wilds I decided to come back and beat the high rank story. I forgot how fun the mechanics are. Being able to zip around with the wirebugs and pull off crazy moves is really engaging. The monster designs and choices are also extremely good, containing what I believe to be some of if not the best designs in the series (it also has my favorite monster, you'll never guess which one :D). Rise has a traditional Japanese theme and as such both the new and returning monsters fit into the theme of Japanese Yo-Kai. The first monster you fight in the game, Great Izuchi is based upon the Kamaitachi a weasel with a sickle tail.
