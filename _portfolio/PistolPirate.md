## Heading 
Created the entire game from scratch using licensed models from the Cosmos. 
 The goal of the assignment was to create a first person game with three levels. The assignment took place over 3 weeks. This was my first solo developed game. The easiest part of the development was the level designing, the assets were pre-made and of high quality so making the level only took about three days. The most difficult part of the development was by far the coding aspect. I am not a coder by trade, most of the time spent on this project was in the lab fixing out various bugs and errors. Some of the bottles spawning sideways, the bullets not moving with the mouse, and the score not being counted were some of the most prominent issues that I can remember. This project comes from the middle of my academic career. It's not as clean as the cookie cutter assignments that came before and not as creative as the projects that came after; but it is the first real game that I made, and by myself at that. In the end, this project will always hold a special place in my heart.
