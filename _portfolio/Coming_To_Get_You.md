## Heading 
Created the animation in blender using free models from blender and audio from George Romero's Night of the Living Dead.
 The assignment was to create a short 15 second video containing two characters and audio. I had a lot of fun with this assignment. This class only had length requirements for the submissions so I could get *really* weird with it (The professor showed concern about my submissions on multiple occasions). I used these models before for a previous assignment in the so I managed to iron all the quirks with their rigging out.
